Founder — Martin Jayapal

Sports Psychologist | Philanthropist | Sports Enthusiast

Martin Jayapal is the visionary behind Rio Sportz Academy, blending sports psychology with a lifelong passion for cricket and football.

  • Captain of Skylab Football Club (est. 1984) — a prestigious Bangalore club, shaping his leadership and teamwork approach.
  • Runs two thriving clubs (cricket & football) offering structured training and mentorship.
  • Sports philanthropist, building platforms for grassroots sports development across India.
“To create a culture of grassroots sports development in India, where every child has the chance to grow in confidence, fitness, and skill through cricket and football.”
